How this was built

1

Discovery & scope

Defined scope with the customer: sites, data sources, compliance needs. Aligned internal teams (sales, success, engineering) and customer stakeholders on timeline, deliverables, and success criteria so everyone had one version of the truth.

2

C-level & stakeholder alignment

Kept C-level and decision-makers aligned on scope and outcomes—no surprises at go-live. Translated technical progress into business impact (e.g. data completeness, time-to-value) so compliance and leadership could see value early.

3

Technical delivery & go-live

Ran implementations as technical and project lead: data/API setup, configuration, training, and cutover. Delivered 700+ sites with ~30% faster time-to-go-live and >95% data completeness at launch by reusing playbooks and keeping risks visible.

4

Proof-of-value → contract

Drove PoV and technical evaluations: demos and trials that showed platform capabilities in the customer’s context. Translated that into business impact for decision-makers; contributed to ~45% PoV-to-contract conversion and $500K qualified pipeline.
